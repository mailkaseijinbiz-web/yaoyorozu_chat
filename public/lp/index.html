How it works

Three Steps to
Summoning.

No setup. No markers to print. Just your phone and the world around you.

01

Scan

Tap the screen to scan any object. AI identifies it and generates a unique spirit — name, personality, and voice — in seconds.

02

Meet

A glowing ring marks the spirit's vessel. Point your camera at the object again to anchor it in AR space.

03

Talk

Switch to Talk mode. Spirits start bantering automatically — speech bubbles and TTS voices included. Tap a spirit to make it speak.
