The Idea · 八百万 (Yaoyorozu)

A Deity in Every
Single Thing.

In Japan, people have long believed that a spirit — a kami — dwells in every object: the mug on your desk, the worn key, the streetlight on your walk home. The word 八百万 (yaoyorozu) — literally "eight million" — means the countless deities woven through everyday life.

YAOYOROZU CHAT brings that ancient idea to life. Point your camera, and the spirit that has always lived inside an object finally speaks.
